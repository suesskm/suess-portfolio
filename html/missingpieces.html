Missing Pieces

"Missing Pieces" is an evolving project that delves into the nostalgia of adolescence and childhood, juxtaposed against the journey of navigating adulthood independently. These prints serve as a visual exploration of past memories and personal transformation. Employing a blend of wood and metal types alongside polymer plates, I intricately crafted each image to evoke a sense of longing and introspection.

I created the "Missing Pieces" project as a means of grappling with the complex emotions and transitions associated with growing up. Drawing from my own experiences and observations, I sought to explore the interplay between the innocence of youth and the challenges of adulthood. Through this creative endeavor, I aimed to capture the essence of nostalgia while reflecting on the transformative journey of self-discovery and resilience. Ultimately, "Missing Pieces" became a personal exploration and expression of these themes, inviting viewers to reflect on their own journeys of growth and introspection.
