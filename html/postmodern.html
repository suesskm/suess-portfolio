postmodernism

Based on my essay, “Ed Fella and the Deconstruction of Typography and Design”, I chose to make the zine in the postmodernist style, while mending that with my style of design as well. Postmodernism, as described in my essay, is a difficult style to narrow down, but I chose to include bright colors, and experimental design strategies in the zine to pay homage to the pioneers of this aesthetic. The zine is a small, pocket -sized book measuring only 4” x 6”.
