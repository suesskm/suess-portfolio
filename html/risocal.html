risograph calendar

This project was a dynamic collaboration within our class, where each student took on the challenge of designing two months of the year for printing on the risograph. Guided by two themes chosen by democratic vote—the Gestalt Principles and the formation of rocks and minerals—we set out to infuse our designs with creativity and meaning. Following printing, the designs were carefully collated and hung on a wire frame, culminating in a visually captivating display measuring 8” x 10”.
